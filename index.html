Thank you for your interest in participating in the WMDMS Member Survey. We have two survey formats available:

Verbose Survey

Comprehensive survey with detailed questions, multiple choice options, rating scales, and open-ended text responses. Best for gathering in-depth feedback and insights.

Start Verbose Survey

Succinct Survey

Streamlined survey with focused open-ended questions. Best for members with limited time who prefer to provide brief, direct responses.

Your feedback matters. Whether you complete the verbose or succinct version, your responses will help us understand your needs and shape the future of the Men's Shed. There are no right or wrong answers—we simply want to hear your voice.
